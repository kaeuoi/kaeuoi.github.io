Objective for Kaeuoi

Kaeuoi can be compared to a labour union, representing the opposite than usual. Thus, instead of having the labour theory of capital as a root of its existence, Kaeuoi has Intequity (Capital of ideas) as a root, combining it with labour. The purpose is for Internet users, to group together against exploitation on the Internet. Lately the news showed clearly, Internet users are exploited, especially by social media websites. Imparting of ideas and personal information is worth a lot, making vast amounts of money from it. Whilst accepting advertising, e.g. Kaeuoi Community will be, ones could say, a migrant community on the Internet, to "greener pastures", if they are not sufficiently respected by the-exploiting. The Kaeuoi Community will use their group power to negotiate better circumstances for the group on the Internet. An example of how group power can be used, is, diverting advertising income to Kaeuoi Community members. If website owners and managers will not be willing to share advertising income with Internet users, the Kaeuoi Community can migrate to other websites, where they are considered more.

Becoming a Member

Kaeuoi will be the social network of Ipparts Exchange (IPPAEX). Groups will form on Kaeuoi representing the brand currencies listed on IPPAEX. For example, one of the first groups will be the fonetones (FON) community. Ownership of the FON brand currency will make a holder of FON an automatic member of the fonetones community at the Kaeuoi social network, unless membership is cancelled. FON holders will have voting rights, equal to their percentage holding of FON. FON holders will also communicate with Africahead, which owns 52% of the fonetones brand. Communication between FON holders and Africahead, will increase the value of the fonetones (FON) brand currency with good new ideas thought of by the community. Africahead will remunerate the community with FON brand tokens, for their good ideas, as individuals and a group.

Choosing idealism as dominant over materialism implies a choice is made for survival with creative ways. It is a choice in favour of the ideas Truth and Love, against idolatry/messianism, in favour of universal laws. It is a choice in favour of groups Being good. Choosing idealism implies considering value added by labour and ideas whilst remunerating accordingly, with for example, bonuses for good ideas.

If the logo is turned around, it refers to others' souls and choosing materialism as opposed to idealism. Materialism basically means, humans are determined by matter and their surrounds. Choosing materialism as dominant over idealism, means a choice is made for survival by murderous means. It is a choice against the ideas Truth and Love, against universal laws. It is a choice in favour of idolatry, messianism, apocalypticism and evil singularity. Choosing materialism means trust in only the labour theory of capital, whilst imparting ideas, without remunerating people for good ideas. The Kaeuoi logo may not be used in this orientation.
