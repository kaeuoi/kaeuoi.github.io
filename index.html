This is the project page of kaeuoi where the website is in development.

The idea for Kaeuoi is as follows: Kaeuoi (KAE) ERC20 brand tokens, on the Ethereum blockchain, will be issued to raise capital for development of a Kaeuoi Community website. The website will develop here on Github. Contributors will be paid from the capital raised by two tokens. KAE brand tokens and Moyom (MYM) utility token.

Currently only the logo and the name makes up the brand value. To follow the developments go to the Initial Token Offering (ITO) page at Africahead.

During the ITO and after the ITO, KAE brand tokens will be listed on Ipparts Exchange (IPPAEX).

Moyom (MYM) utility token will be used as the money for the Kaeuoi Community.

Kaeuoi logo, in the above orientation, refers to one's own soul and comprehension of idealism as opposed to materialism.
