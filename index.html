What is Kaeuoi

A Community That Owns Its Value

Kaeuoi is best understood as a new kind of labour union — one rooted not in the labour theory of capital, but in Intequity: the capital of ideas combined with labour. Its purpose is to unite Internet users against exploitation, especially by social media platforms that profit from their members' ideas and personal information without fair compensation.

The Kaeuoi Community uses collective power to negotiate better circumstances for its members — including the redirection of advertising income back to the people who create the value in the first place.

🤝

Collective Power

Members pool their influence to negotiate fairer treatment from platforms and advertisers.

💡

Intequity

Ideas are capital. Kaeuoi recognises and rewards the intellectual contributions of its members.

🌍

Migration Rights

If a platform fails to respect the community, Kaeuoi members can migrate together to better pastures.

📢

Shared Revenue

Advertising income diverted to community members — your attention has real worth.

How to Join

Becoming a Member

Kaeuoi will be the social network of Ipparts Exchange (IPPAEX). Membership is automatic for holders of any brand currency listed on IPPAEX. Groups form around each brand currency, giving holders voting rights proportional to their percentage holding — creating a genuine ownership stake in the community.

For example, holders of fonetones (FON) automatically join the fonetones community on Kaeuoi, where they can communicate with Africahead — which holds 52% of the FON brand — and contribute ideas that grow the brand's value. Good ideas are rewarded with additional FON tokens.

Token Offering

KAE · Capital for Development

Kaeuoi (KAE) ERC-20 brand tokens on the Ethereum blockchain are being issued to raise capital for the development of the Kaeuoi Community. During and after the ITO, KAE tokens will be listed on IPPAEX. Moyom (MYM) utility tokens will serve as the internal currency of the Kaeuoi Community.

Brand Identity

The Kaeuoi Logo

In its correct orientation the Kaeuoi logo symbolises one's own soul and the comprehension of idealism over materialism. Idealism holds that humans structure matter starting with good ideas — a choice in favour of Truth and Love, of creativity and universal laws, of rewarding labour and ideas alike.

The inverted orientation represents the opposite: materialism, determination by external forces, and the suppression of ideas without remuneration. The inverted logo may not be used.
